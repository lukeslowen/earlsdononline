About Earlsdon Online

Coventry's longest running community website — since 1995

Earlsdon Online was created by Earlsdon resident Dave Galliers in 1995 and is Coventry's longest running community website. What began as a simple local resource has grown over three decades into a comprehensive record of Earlsdon's history, community life, and local information.

Website Development

Over the years this website has been redesigned and refreshed a number of times. More recently the site has been revised using Claude AI.

Additional website development and support is provided by Earlsdon resident Luke Slowen of Fireside Media.

Contributing

Much of the historical content on this site has been researched and contributed by members of the local community over many years. We are always grateful to receive historic photographs, documents, or stories about Earlsdon. If you have something to share, please get in touch.

For news and local discussions, visit the Earlsdon Local Message Board on Facebook, or follow the Earlsdon History page for historic photos and stories.
