EARLSDON
AREA OF LOCAL DISTINCTIVENESS

 

  

The Zones



 

 

   The ALD Working Group have already undertaken a pilot study of the Earlsdon area. The area is actually comprised of many different sub-areas dating from different periods and reflecting many different architectural styles. This in itself gives the area its unique character. This pilot study divides Earlsdon into the following study areas or zones:
