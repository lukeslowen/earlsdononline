Neighbourhood and representative community groups

Earlsdon is a very active community and this is reflected in the number of community groups in the area. Among these are a variety of representative neighbourhood groups which are working with the City Council, elected representatives and other agencies to protect the area and develop improved provision for local people.

These groups also work together as the 'Earlsdon Community Network'. View the Earlsdon Community Network in a map.
