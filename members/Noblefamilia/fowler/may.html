May Fowler

1905 – 1994

May was born on 5 January 1905 at 107 Manvers Street, Sneinton, Nottingham. One of seven children, her father John Whitaker died in 1909 when May was just four.

May's brother Bertie was killed in the First World War when she was 12. In March 1923, aged 18, May married William Croome but the marriage failed.

Around 1926 May met William (Bill) Drury. Their daughter Mavis was born in 1928. After May's divorce, she and Bill married in 1930. The family returned to Nottingham in 1933. May died in 1994 aged 89.
