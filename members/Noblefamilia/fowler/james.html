James Fowler

b. 1870, Bedford

James Fowler was born in Bedford in 1870. His family later moved to Nottingham, settling in Sneinton, where James worked as a painter for the railway company.

James married Eliza Peck in October 1891. He left the family in 1896, leading to Eliza forming a relationship with John Whitaker.
