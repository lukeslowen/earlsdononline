Henrietta Marshall

b. c.1855, Nottingham

Henrietta Marshall was born in Nottingham around 1855. Her family were part of Nottingham's textile industry.

Henrietta married Charles Marsh in Nottingham in 1880. Through her daughter Edith, who married George Noble, the Marshall name becomes woven into the Noble family story.
