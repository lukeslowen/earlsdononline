Tom Drury

1869 – 1917

Tom Drury was born in Nottingham in 1869. He enlisted in the British Army and served in the 9th South Staffordshires and later the Labour Corps, seeing action at Ypres and on the Western Front.

Tom Drury died in 1917 from wounds sustained in action. His military records have been extensively researched, including Army Forms, Military History Sheet, and accounts of his service in France and Belgium.

Tom married and had children including William (Bill) Drury, who also served in the First World War.
