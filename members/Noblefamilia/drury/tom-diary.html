Tom Drury's Diary

First World War diary and records

Tom Drury's military records include Army Form B103, B111, W5080, Form B178, his Military History Sheet, and Labour Corps Records.

These documents together paint a picture of a working man's experience of the Great War — from enlistment through deployment to France and Belgium, service at Ypres, and his death from wounds in 1917.
