Tom Drury — South Staffordshire Regiment

Tom Drury and the South Staffordshire Regiment/Labour Corp

This is a record of the military service of <a href="tom.html">Tom Drury</a> as recorded through unit histories and battalion diaries from the First World War. He served for most of the war in the 9th Battalion of the South Staffordshire Regiment. In 1917 he was transferred to the 1/5 South Staffs and shortly after that the Labour Corp. The account is divided into the main periods of service.
