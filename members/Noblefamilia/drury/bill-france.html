Bill Drury — France & Flanders

The Western Front, 1916–1918

Bill Drury crossed to France in early 1916 with the 11th Battalion Leicestershire Regiment. His diary records the crossing from Le Havre and the march up to the front line.

He served through 1916, 1917, and 1918, describing life in the trenches, billets behind the lines, and the landscape of war-torn northern France and Belgium.
