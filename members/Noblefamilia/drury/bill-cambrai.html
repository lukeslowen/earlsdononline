Battle of Cambrai

November–December 1917

The Battle of Cambrai (20 November – 7 December 1917) was one of the first major uses of tanks in warfare. The 11th Leicestershire Regiment, in which Bill Drury served, took part in this significant engagement.

Bill's diary records the lead-up to the battle and its aftermath — one of the most intense periods of his service.
