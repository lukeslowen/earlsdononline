Bill Drury — Ireland, 1914–15

Ireland with the Leicestershire Regiment, 1914–15

At the outbreak of war in August 1914, Bill Drury and the Leicestershire Regiment were stationed in Ireland, based at the Curragh and various barracks around the country.

Bill's diary records training in Ireland, the tensions of the period, and the eventual departure for the Western Front.
