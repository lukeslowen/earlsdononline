Army Form W5080

Army Form W5080 — Statement of Names and Addresses of Relatives.

From: Colonel i/c Labour Corps Records, Nottingham

To: Mrs Lucy Drury, Newlands, Leicester Road, Shepshed

In order that I may be enabled to dispose of the plaque and scroll in commemoration of the soldier named below, I shall be glad if you will fill in the form on the reverse and return it to me as soon as possible.

Pte. Tom Drury, deceased

Statement of the names and addresses of all relatives of the above-named deceased soldier:

Widow of the Soldier: Lucy Drury

Children of the Soldier: William Drury (b. November 11, 1897); Eva (b. December 25, 1901); Lyn (b. December 29, 1917)

Father of the Soldier: None

Mother of the Soldier: None

Brothers of the Soldier (Full Blood): John Drury, Fenton; Charles Drury, 12 Kirkby St, Lincoln; William Drury, 3 Greenfield Street, Manchester

Brothers of the Soldier (Half Blood): Norris Drury

Sisters of the Soldier (Full Blood): Polly Drury
