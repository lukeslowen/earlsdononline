Eliza Peck

b. c.1866, Nottingham

Eliza Peck was born in Nottingham around 1866, a descendant of the Peck family of Sutton in Ashfield.

Eliza married James Fowler in October 1891. After James left in 1896, Eliza raised her children in Sneinton, Nottingham with John Whitaker, their children including May Fowler.

Eliza was a remarkable woman who kept her family together as a single parent in difficult circumstances.
