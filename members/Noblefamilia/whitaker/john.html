John Whitaker

b. c.1818, Mansfield Woodhouse

John Whitaker was born in Mansfield Woodhouse around 1818. He took his mother's married name Whitaker.

John formed a relationship with Eliza Fowler (née Peck) in Nottingham and together they had three children. As Eliza was still married to James Fowler, John and Eliza could not wed. He was a devoted partner and father until his death in 1909.

Their children included May Fowler — who later married Bill Drury — making John Whitaker the maternal grandfather of Mavis Drury.
