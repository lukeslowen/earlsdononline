Wood

Surname Origin: Originally describing someone who lived in or worked in a wood or forest, from Middle English "wode". An ancient Scottish surname, first called De Bosco.

Our earliest Wood ancestors have been traced to Lincolnshire. Richard Wood, born in 1761, is the earliest member we can identify directly.

The Wood family connect to the Drury family through Housham Grange. See: Housham Grange.
