Laura's Bohemian Ancestry

A separate branch: Tracing family roots through Central Europe — Austria, Bohemia and the German-speaking world of the 19th century.

This branch of the family tree traces the ancestry of Laura Noble through Central European roots — a fascinating journey through Bohemia, Austria, and the German-speaking world of the 19th and early 20th centuries.

The families documented here include the Niessler, Reinesl, Sturzl, Raab, Wiesner, Fladerer, Kulhan, Schneider, Poshko, Reim, Pohner, Lohwasser, Jaksch, and Knaut families — each with their own stories of life in the Austro-Hungarian Empire.
