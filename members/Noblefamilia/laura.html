Laura's Bohemian Ancestry

Surname Origin: A parallel family tree tracing ancestry through Central Europe — Austria, Bohemia and the German-speaking world of the 19th and early 20th centuries.

This branch of the family tree traces the ancestry of Laura Noble through Central European roots — a fascinating journey through Bohemia, Austria, and the German-speaking world.

The families documented here include the Niessler, Reinesl, Sturzl, Raab, Wiesner, Fladerer, Kulhan, Schneider, Poshko, Reim, Pohner, Lohwasser, Jaksch, and Knaut families — each with their own stories of life in the Austro-Hungarian Empire.

The original research pages for this branch are preserved from the earlier website. Contact us for more information about the Bohemian ancestry research.
