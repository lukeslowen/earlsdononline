Family History

Warry

Surname Origin: The origin remains a mystery, but one theory suggests Cornish/Welsh origin meaning "of an important place".

Our Warrys are deeply rooted in Somerset around the town of Chard. The first directly identifiable ancestor is Stephen Warry of Chard who died in 1530.

Rebecca Warry married James Fowler (senior) in 1863, bringing the Warry and Fowler families together.
