Whitaker

Surname Origin: Habitational name from places named with Old English hwit "white" + æcer "cultivated land" — meaning "white field".

Our Whitaker family dates from John Whitaker, born in Mansfield Woodhouse in about 1818.

John formed a relationship with Eliza Fowler (née Peck) in Nottingham and together they had three children, including May Fowler.
