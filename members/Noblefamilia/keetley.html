Keetley

Surname Origin: An Anglo-Saxon name derived from the settlement of Keighley in West Yorkshire.

John Keetley was born in Nottingham in 1801. He married Hannah and they had four children. By 1841 they were living at Tyler Street, Nottingham. Their son Christopher was born in Nottingham in 1832 and by the age of 18 was working as a foundryman.

Christopher Keetley married Elizabeth Burton in 1853 and they had eight children. Their daughter Mary Ann Keetley is the family member who connects the Keetley line to the Noble family tree.
