Keetley

Surname Origin: An Anglo-Saxon name derived from the settlement of Keighley in West Yorkshire.

John Keetley was born in Nottingham in 1801. Their son Christopher Keetley married Elizabeth Burton in 1853.

Their daughter Mary Ann Keetley connects the Keetley line to the Noble family tree.
