41st Regiment of Foot

Military history of Thomas Noble

Thomas Noble served in the 41st Regiment of Foot during the early 19th century, a regiment that served in various campaigns including the Napoleonic Wars.

The 41st Regiment (later the Welsh Regiment) was raised in 1719. Thomas's service forms part of the military thread running through several branches of this family tree.
