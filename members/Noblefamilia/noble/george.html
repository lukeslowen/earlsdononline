George Noble

b. c.1895, Nottingham

George Noble was born in Nottingham in the late Victorian era. He married Edith Marsh — daughter of Charles Marsh and Henrietta Marshall — in the 1920s.

The couple lived at 5 Guy's Terrace, Blue Bell Hill Road, Nottingham. They had three children: Ken, Ray, and Miriam.

Edith died tragically young, leaving George to raise the children with the help of his sister Hetti and her husband Joe Kirkham.
