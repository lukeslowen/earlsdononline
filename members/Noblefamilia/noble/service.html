Ken Noble — Naval Service

Royal Navy, 1942–1946

Ken Noble served in the Royal Navy during the Second World War, aboard HMS Rochester (a Black Swan-class sloop), HMS Boxer, and HMS Formidable (an aircraft carrier).

Ken served in the East Indies Fleet and was present in the Far East during the final months of the war against Japan, witnessing the Japanese surrender in 1945.

He returned home to Nottingham after demobilisation in 1946.
