Guy's Terrace

Blue Bell Hill Road, Nottingham

Guy's Terrace on Blue Bell Hill Road, Nottingham, was the Noble family home for several decades in the early 20th century — a typical Victorian working-class row of houses.

Ken Noble was born here in 1925 and the house was home to the Noble family through the inter-war years.
