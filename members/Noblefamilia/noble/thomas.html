Thomas Noble

b. c.1760, Chesterfield, Derbyshire

Thomas Noble was born around 1760, descending from the Noble family line that settled in Chesterfield, Derbyshire in the 17th century. The line traces back to John Noble who married Hannah Lee in Chesterfield in February 1655.

Thomas married Mary Ann Bunting and they established their family in Nottingham. Their descendants include several generations documented throughout this family history.

See also the history of Thomas Noble's service in the 41st Regiment of Foot.
