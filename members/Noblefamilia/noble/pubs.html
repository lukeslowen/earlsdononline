Ken Noble's Nottingham Pubs

Nottingham pub trade, 1950s–1980s

After his naval service, Ken Noble built a career in Nottingham's pub trade, working with Shipstones Brewery — the famous Nottingham brewery whose ales were a fixture of local life for generations.

Ken was licensee of several Nottingham pubs including the Wheatsheaf, known as much for his drumming as his landlordship.
