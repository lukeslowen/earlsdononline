Phillip Warren

b. c.1801–1805, Dublin

Phillip Warren was born in Dublin between 1801 and 1805, son of Thomas Warren, a Framework Knitter. The family were Catholic, suggesting Irish origins.

He married Deborah Jelley around 1826. The 1851 census shows the family in England, having emigrated likely driven by the Great Famine.
