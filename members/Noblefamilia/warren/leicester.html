Warren of Leicestershire

Leicestershire branch

The first reference to our Warren family is in Ratcliffe Culey, Leicestershire with the marriage of Thomas Warren to Ann Winters in October 1735.

John Warren established himself as a wheelwright and coachmaker in Ashby de la Zouch and later Loughborough, building one of the town's notable coachmaking enterprises.

Lucy Warren, a descendant, married Tom Drury, connecting the Leicestershire Warrens to the Noble family tree.
