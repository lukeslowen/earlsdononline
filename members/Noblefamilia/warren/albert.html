Albert Warren

b. c.1870

Albert Warren was a member of the Leicestershire Warren family. He served in the British Army including the Tirah Campaign on the North-West Frontier of India (1897–98) and later in the Boer War.

Albert's military service is documented through his service records, medals including the Tirah Campaign Medal and the Meritorious Service Medal, and letters home.
