Lucy Warren

b. c.1871, Loughborough

Lucy Warren was born in Loughborough around 1871, a member of the Leicestershire Warren family who had established themselves as coachmakers in the town.

Lucy married Tom Drury in Nottingham, connecting the Warren and Drury family lines.
