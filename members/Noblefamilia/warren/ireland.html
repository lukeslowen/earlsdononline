Warren of Ireland

Dublin branch

Our Warren branch in Ireland traces to Dublin in the early 19th century. Phillip Warren was born in Dublin between 1801 and 1805, son of Thomas Warren, a Framework Knitter.

Phillip married Deborah Jelley around 1826. The family emigrated from Ireland to England, settling in Nottingham.
