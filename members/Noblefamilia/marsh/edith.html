Edith Marsh

b. c.1896, Nottingham

Edith Marsh was born in Nottingham around 1896, daughter of Charles Marsh and Henrietta Marshall.

Edith married George Noble in Nottingham in the 1920s. They lived at 5 Guy's Terrace, Nottingham, and had three children including Ken Noble.

Edith died young, leaving George to raise the children. Her early death had a profound effect on young Ken Noble.
