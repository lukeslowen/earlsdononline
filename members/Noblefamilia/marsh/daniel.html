Daniel Marsh

b. 1795, Sheffield

Daniel Marsh was born in Sheffield in 1795, christened in Sheffield parish church (later Sheffield Cathedral).

He moved to Liverpool (via the United States) where he practised as a Butcher, then by 1848 the family were in Kingston upon Hull where his son Charles was born. The family moved to Nottingham by 1858, where Daniel died in 1868.
