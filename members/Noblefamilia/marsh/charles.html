Charles Marsh

b. c.1850, Kingston upon Hull

Charles Marsh was born in Kingston upon Hull around 1850, son of Daniel Marsh. After serving in the army, he returned to Nottingham in 1877 and found employment as a lace maker.

He married Henrietta Marshall in 1880. Their daughter Edith Marsh later married George Noble.
