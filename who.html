North Earlsdon Neighbourhood Association

WHo's WHO

 

A 'Who's Who' of NENA and how to get involved. Who are the Committee and the Street contacts.

NENA was formed in 2009 and exists to protect and enhance our environment and promote a strong sense of community across our neighbourhood. Membership now stands over 400 households out of a potential 600 homes. This is over two thirds of the residents in the North Earlsdon area.

See 'About us' for a more information and a map of the NENA area.

NENA holds full Members Meetings every two months and membership is free. We have an elected committee, their names are below, but we are organised around street contacts who ensure that everyone is kept fully informed. However, it's not just about meetings and you do not need to have a formal role to be involved. If you would like to put something back into your local community or if you have a special interest please get in touch.

If you would like to get involved, have your say or simply be kept informed, just send your name and address to nena.earlsdon@gmail.com .
