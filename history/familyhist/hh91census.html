1891 Census – Full Transcript

Transcript of Piece RG12/2451 · Earlsdon, Coventry

Transcript of Piece RG12/2451. This is a full transcript of the 1891 Census showing all residents of Earlsdon at that time. Census schedule numbers are shown — these are not house numbers. Transcriber’s annotations are noted where present, and linked biographies are highlighted. Use the search box to find names, addresses or occupations.

A suburb transformed. By 1891 Earlsdon had grown dramatically. The old watchmaking trade was giving way to the emerging bicycle industry — many households record both occupations side by side. The streets now extended well beyond the old Earlsdon Lane axis, and Albany Road would open just seven years later to complete the suburb we recognise today.
