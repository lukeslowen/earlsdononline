1851 Census Transcript

Transcript of Piece H107/2067 · Coventry St Michael, District 34

Transcript of Piece H107/2067, Coventry St Michael, District 34. This is a transcript of the 1851 Census showing all the residents of Earlsdon at that time. Use the search box to find names, addresses or occupations. Census schedule numbers are shown — these are not house numbers.

A village on the cusp of change. In 1851 Earlsdon was still largely rural — just a handful of households strung along Elsdon Lane (now Earlsdon Avenue South), with the White House inn, a few farms, and the Mercer family's watch manufactory at Broomfields. Within a decade the watchmaking community would transform the area completely, as recorded in the 1861 Census.
