1861 Census – Full Transcript

Transcript of Piece RG9/2205 · Coventry St Michael, District 35

Transcript of Piece RG9/2205, Coventry St Michael, District 35. This is a full transcript of the 1861 Census showing all residents of Earlsdon at that time. Census schedule numbers are shown — these are not house numbers. Transcriber’s annotations are noted where present, and linked biographies are highlighted. Use the search box to find names, addresses or occupations.

The watchmakers’ village. By 1861 Earlsdon had transformed from the quiet farmland of the 1851 Census into a bustling settlement of watchmakers, ribbon weavers and their families. Many residents had migrated from Lancashire — particularly Prescot, the other great English watchmaking town — drawn by work in Coventry’s expanding trade.
