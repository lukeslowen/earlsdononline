Spennell's 1912 Street Directory

Extract of Earlsdon residents from Spennell's 1912 Directory

Extracts from Spennell's Directory listing the residents of Earlsdon in 1912. Of particular interest to current residents are the original house names that have been forgotten over the years. Please note that in some cases house numbers have changed since 1912.

The full directory covered the following Earlsdon streets. The original scanned pages of this directory are held at the Coventry History Centre.
