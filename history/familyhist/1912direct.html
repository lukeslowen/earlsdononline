Spennell's 1912 Street Directory

Extract of Earlsdon residents from Spennell's 1912 Directory

Scanned pages from Spennell's 1912 Directory listing the residents of Earlsdon streets. Of particular interest are the original house names that have since been forgotten. Please note that in some cases house numbers have changed since 1912.
