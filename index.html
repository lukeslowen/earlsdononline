About Earlsdon

A neighbourhood with deep roots

Earlsdon Online is a community resource for people living in the Earlsdon area of Coventry. It is intended as a gateway to local information, history, and community life.

From its origins as 30 acres of farmland bought in 1851, Earlsdon grew into one of Coventry's most desirable residential districts. With little redevelopment over the decades, it has kept much of its original character.

Browse our history section to discover the fascinating stories of the people and places that have shaped Earlsdon, or join the conversation on the Earlsdon Local Message Board on Facebook.
