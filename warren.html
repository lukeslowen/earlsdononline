Warren

Surname Origin: From Guarenna or Varenna in Normandy — they came to England with William the Conqueror. The primary sense is to guard or keep off.

We have two very separate Warren family branches in our family which, as far as we know, are unconnected. One appears well established in Leicestershire and were a family of Coachbuilders who eventually settled in Loughborough. The other originates from Ireland and left Ireland before the 'Famine Years' to settle in Nottingham, England.
